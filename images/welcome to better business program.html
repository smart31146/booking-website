Better Business Program
DEMB's Code of Conduct sets out our commitment to behaving legally, ethically and with integrity in everything we do. It is about doing the right thing and ensuring we can be proud of our organisation, and our values.

As part of your Business and Ethical Principles Compliance training requirements as a D.E Coffee & Tea employee (with online access), in addition to programs issued by DEMB, you are required to complete a number of short online courses each year to gain accreditation in the particular components and these are designated by your General Manager / Director.

This email is an invitation to complete our online Better Business Program. Completing this program is mandatory for all employees in our business. Some employees need to complete different designated modules within the program, depending upon their role. The program will show you the subjects and quizzes that you need to complete. Satisfactory completion (as reflected in a "pass") of each designated quiz is required as part of your annual final appraisal and a prerequisite for any bonus entitlements (where applicable). Results are reviewed by the Compliance Committee so please ensure you complete each designated quiz.

Please note that you should not re-enter a program once it has been successfully completed as the system will note your entry and you may be recorded as a failure (not having completed the program on that occasion).
